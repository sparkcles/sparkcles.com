About Sparkcles

My name is Camila Sanchez and I am a self taught artist from Argentina. I have been experimenting with mediums since I was a little girl. Drawing inspiration from life, my work reflects the magic in every day. The wonder of our world captivates and motivates me. My color pallette is bright and energetic, with pastel tones. Gratitude is a big theme among my pieces, reflecting on the loving relationship between ourselves, our loved ones, and our world. My heart is always open inviting inspiration.

"Life is art, a beautiful dance of stardust and light, every particle dancing to the beat of love".

What I do

commissions of all shapes and sizes including murals and even houses. Give me your weirdest idea and I'll bring it to life.
